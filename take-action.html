Ways to get involved

- If you are an expectant parent choosing natural/home birth and you are interested in having your birthing experience delicately and sensitively caught on camera, you can book a consultation with me to connect and further discuss the details

- Offer your skills and services to support the exhibition; as this is a charitable project donations of time and energy to help make it a success are invaluable. You can find a list of skills and services that would be most helpful below, but please get in touch if you feel moved to get involved even if your specific skills aren't on the list

skills: organisational, social media marketing, promoting

Services: music/sound system, catering, venue, flowers, printing

- Book photoshoot and 20% of the cost will go towards the fundraising

- Spread the word on social media - please like and share the link to this project far and wide!
